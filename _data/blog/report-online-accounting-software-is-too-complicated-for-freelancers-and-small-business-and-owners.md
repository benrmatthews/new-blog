Is [online accounting software](https://freetrain.co/best-online-accounting-software/) for freelancers and small business owners too difficult to learn and use?

That what [FreshBooks](https://shareasale.com/r.cfm?b=1391906&u=1840782&m=52946&urllink=&afftrack=) asked when they surveyed 300 North American-based accountants and bookkeepers with small business clients were asked their opinions about online accounting software for freelancers and small business owners.

71% of respondents to the survey – the clear majority – said that their clients used QuickBooks.

Survey Results: Online Accounting Software is Too Complicated
-------------------------------------------------------------

The research found that:

1. Almost 68% say small business owners find it intimidating
2. Almost 69% say small business owners find it difficult to learn
3. 83% agree it’s typical for small business owners to make mistakes when using it
4. 2 out of 3 believe small business owners require specialised accounting skills or experience to use it

<span>[FreshBooks](https://shareasale.com/r.cfm?b=1391906&u=1840782&m=52946&urllink=&afftrack=) surveyed more than 300 North American-based accountants and bookkeepers online in January of 2019. Respondents were asked their opinions about small business accounting software. The study’s margin of error is +/- 5.5% at 95% confidence.</span>
